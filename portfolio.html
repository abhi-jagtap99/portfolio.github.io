About Me

Frontend Developer

Hello and welcome to my portfolio!
    My name is Abhijit Jagtap and I am a frontend web developer based in Pandharpur. I am passionate about creating beautiful, user-friendly websites and web applications that help businesses and individuals achieve their goals.
    I first became interested in web development during my undergraduate studies, where I studied computer science. From there, I started learning more about HTML, CSS and JavaScript on my own and fell in love with the creative and technical aspects of building websites.
    Over the past few months, I have worked on various web development projects including social media sites, e-commerce sites and blogs. I am proficient in several frontend technologies including React, Angular and Bootstrap and am always keen to learn new tools and techniques to improve my skills.
    If you are interested in working together on a web development project, please don't hesitate to get in touch! Thanks for visiting my portfolio!
